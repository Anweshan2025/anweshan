About Anweshan

Welcome to Anweshan, where imagination meets innovation.

Here, science isn’t just about formulas and facts — it’s about turning your imagination into reality. We give you a platform to express your creativity, whether it’s through Rangoli making, poster design, or exhibiting your science projects.

Whether you’re building, experimenting, or dreaming up something entirely new, this is your stage to shine. ✨ Sign up for Anweshan 2025 today — it’s never too late for science!

Rules & Guidelines

1. Team Composition & Project Type

Each group must have 4 participants. Projects should be science-based — working or non-working models are both acceptable.

2. Exhibition Rotation

During the exhibition, 2 students will explain for 2 hours while the other 2 take a break. Teams will alternate roles throughout the day.

3. Preparation & Knowledge

Participants must have complete knowledge of their exhibit. Only well-prepared models and presenters will be considered.

4. Elimination Round

An elimination round will be held after the Diwali Vacation (3rd week of November).

Enrollment: Scroll down to register by 7th November 2025.
